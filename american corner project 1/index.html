JIMMA AMERICAN CORNER

The Jimma American Corner is located at the Jimma city public library in partnership with the Jimma city Culture and Tourism Bureau.
The Corner enjoys visits and program attendance from the nearby high schools and Jimma University and offers various technologies including
video conferencing, a green screen, iPads, and makerspace tools.
